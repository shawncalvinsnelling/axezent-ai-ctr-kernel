# Claim Boundary

Axezent AI CTR is a deterministic finite-receipt checker.

Accepted public claim:

> Given a submitted finite receipt and a declared policy pack, Axezent AI CTR verifies whether the receipt obeys the deterministic rules in that policy pack.

Rejected overclaims:

- The project does not prove that an AI is globally truthful.
- The project does not prove that an AI is fully aligned.
- The project does not verify hidden model reasoning.
- The project does not guarantee all future model outputs are safe.
- The project does not guarantee all software is bug-free.

Use the phrase:

```text
No claim without a receipt.
```
