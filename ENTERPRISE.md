# Enterprise Direction

Axezent AI CTR is open source at the receipt/checker level.

Enterprise offerings can be built around:

- signed compliance certificates
- hosted receipt vaults
- dashboard review workflows
- custom policy packs
- private deployment support
- agent integration adapters
- streaming verification infrastructure

Enterprise truth boundary:

Paid systems certify finite receipts under declared deterministic policy packs. They do not certify global AI truthfulness or unrestricted model safety.
