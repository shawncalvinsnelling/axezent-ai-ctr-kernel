# Axezent AI CTR Commercial / Enterprise Options

Axezent AI CTR is open source under the MIT License.

Paid enterprise services can be built around the same receipt format and checker architecture:

- signed compliance certificates
- private audit vaults
- hosted receipt dashboards
- custom policy packs
- private deployment support
- AI-agent integration support
- security review and training
- high-throughput streaming runtime integrations

Truth boundary: enterprise services certify finite receipts under declared deterministic policy packs. They do not certify that an AI model is globally truthful, fully aligned, or safe in every future context.
